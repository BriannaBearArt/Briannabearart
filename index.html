Recent Project

skʷeq̓əŋ - Flower In Lekwugen, the word for flower is skʷeq̓ə (shh-quay-kwa), flower. Some Elders I have heard use this word to refer to our young children that are our little flowers. They are the ones blooming with opportunity and the ones to carry on our ways. But when we think of every child matters, we think of the love we couldn’t give the children in Residential School; we think about the teachings they missed out on, and the chance to bloom like a beautiful camas they were to be. To honor our lands and our flowers, I wanted to add camas and a mother like figure holding the child in their arms surrounded by love to everyone effected by Residential Schools and to our communities raising our next generation with the utmost love.
